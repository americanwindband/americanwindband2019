Supporting music education...

We wish to thank you for your interest in our scholarship competition. One high school finalist will be awarded a $1,800.00 scholarship, and another high school student will receive a $1,600 scholarship. One 8th grade finalist will be awarded a $1,500.00 scholarship, and another 8th grade student will receive a $1,100 scholarship. The scholarship money will be paid to each student’s private teacher for future study on a band instrument or the band camp of the student’s choosing.

We have a two-step process. Applicants will provide an unaccompanied audio only recordings of their solos for the first round of the competition. Judges will only consider the quality of the recorded performance but not the quality of the media used.

Based upon the first-round recordings, the scholarship committee will select contestants to compete in the second round. The second round is scheduled to take place in person on Saturday, April 2, 2022, at Gloria Dei Lutheran Church, 4501 Main Street, Downers Grove, Illinois.
